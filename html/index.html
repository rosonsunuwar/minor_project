YAMAHA

Owning bikes in Nepal has become a must in Nepal. Hectic traffic jams, narrow streets and unreliable public transportation has made bike a basic need to everyone. Bikes are less expensive than car, easy to park and can be used to transport everything and everyone. These are the reasons that make bikes in Nepal a necessity.

Bikes mean basically motorcycles and scooters. In Nepal, Yamaha bikes are considered as one of the most trusted ones. In fact Yamaha bikes are most technologically advanced and are loaded with the features that make riding a pleasure.

MAW Enterprises started its operation with Yamaha since 1975 importing motorcycles in Nepal. At that time, we introduced Rajdoot and Escorts bikes which are now Yamaha. Since then we have been offering dozens of model to Nepalese customers. We have nation-wide network of over 100 dealers. Presently, our product portfolio includes, sports models such as R15V4 (155cc), R15V3 (155cc), Neo-Retro Model FZ-X (149cc), Blue-Core Technology enabled models such as FZS-FI V3 BS6 X-connect (149)cc, FZ-S FI (Fuel-Injected, 149 cc), FZ FI (Fuel-Injected, 149 cc), SZ-RR version 2.0 (149 cc), Saluto (125 cc), Fuel efficient with High Performance Scooter RayZR 125 FI (125cc), Fascino (125 cc & 113 cc), Ray Z (113 cc), Ray ZR (113 cc). And these are well celebrated bikes in Nepal.

Yamaha’s motorcycles and scooters are fitted with patented ‘BLUE CORE’ engines. R15, the one which introduced racing culture in Nepal has its design and engine adopted from the racing beast R1. It is tremendously popular among youngsters with racing instinct. FZ, R15 in bike and RayZR 125 FI, Fascino 125 FI in scooter are equipped with Fuel Injection (FI) technology that not only enhances the performance of motorcycle but also lessens the fuel consumption and thus increasing the mileage.

Corporate office of Yamaha Division is located in Tripureshwor, Kathmandu. Nepal’s first YAMAHA Bike Station (YBS) is located at Tripureshwor, Kathmandu. YBS has a showroom, service workshop, and a spares i.e. 3S outlet all under the same roof and is the largest center of bikes in Nepal.

We have over 100 dealers all over nations with facility of sale, service and spare parts. Besides, we have more than one hundred Authorized Service Points (ASPs) in different place of the country, so that customer can reach their nearest service center. We organize free service camp every month in different place of the country as well.
